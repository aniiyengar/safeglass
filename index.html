SafeGlass

Safe, efficient travel comes to Google Glass

DEMO

A constant travel companion

In large, often congested cities like Detroit, movement is cumbersome and information is elusive. SafeGlass solves both problems. It presents important information from crime statistics to parking locations, and does so in an intuitive way. We used the internal magnetic field detector and gyroscope in Google Glass to bring an old problem—safe and efficient city transportation—to the frontiers of augmented reality.
